    GUARANTEE POLICY

    When purchasing products at BKAAK.com.vn, customers have the right to be completely assured with genuine products, receive an official warranty at the company and in addition have a free exchange policy of up to 30 days at BKAAK.

    Product type Free renewal Regulations on re-entry and return
    Phone/ Tablet/ Macbook/ Apple watch 30 days During the first 30 days of re-importing the device, a 20% fee will be deducted from the current price (or purchase price if the purchase price is lower than the current price).- After 30 days, re-import the machine at the agreed price

    Return conditions:

    • Machine: Like new, no scratches, no decals or decorations
    • Used machine: the product is in the same condition as when it was first purchased
    • Box: Like new, not dented, torn, broken, written, drawn, wrapped with tape or glue; Serial/IMEI on the box matches the camera body.
    • Accessories and gifts: Complete, intact, not dented, scratched or damaged during use.
    • Account: The device has been logged out of all accounts such as: iCloud, Google Account, Mi Account...

    Errors from the NSX need to be determined by a genuine warranty center or an authorized warranty center
    (*) Errors from the Manufacturer are errors including: Power errors, errors on the mainboard, hard drive, screen and internal hardware components
    Screen dead pixel error: screen has 3 or more dead spots or 1 dead spot larger than 1mm in size for phones and 5 or more dead spots for laptops and separate screens

Company law. In case the Customer does not provide all the above documents, CellphoneS would like to collect service fees according to the VAT value of the returned product.
