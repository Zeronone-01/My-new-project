    Warranty Policy At PKAAK


    • Extended standard warranty policy at PKAAK

    • PKAAK will warranty in accordance with Apple warranty regulations for up to 24 additional months for your device. After the device's 1-year genuine warranty period expires,PKAAK offers the following warranty according to the extended package you own..

      Applicable products: iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch, MacBook, iMac, Mac Mini products at PKAAK system.

      Warranty period applicable at PKAAK:

      Package (Dcare+6T): Duration 6 months (Starting from the 13th month after the customer purchases at the PKAAK system) Package (Dcare+12T): Duration 12 months (Starting from the 13th month after the customer purchases at the PKAAK system) Package (Dcare+24T): Duration 24 months (Starting from the 13th month after the customer purchases at the PKAAK system)

      Warranty benefits: PKAAK overed by warranty: Component errors due to the manufacturer:
      Screen, Main, Camera, Speaker, Mic...


    • APPLE's standard warranty policy

    1. All products purchased at the nationwide PKAAK store system and at the website https://PKAAK.com/ are covered by the warranty policy in accordance with Apple's regulations.

    2. The warranty program takes effect from the moment PKAAK invoices you.

    3. Each product line has a different warranty policy according to the company's regulations.

    4. Customers need to present the purchase invoice to receive warranty.

      5. Warranty period:

      Free 1 for 1 exchange within the first 30 days if the product is defective due to the manufacturer (Machine has no scratches, dents,...) Warranty according to Apple's policy for products used from the 2nd month to the 12th month if errors arise due to the manufacturer. Standard 1-year warranty on accessories.


    • Is something broken? Replace it immediately!!!(NOT applicable to Apple Watch)

    • If the product is damaged, it can be exchanged (same model, same capacity, same color...) for a genuine product and equivalent accessories, specifically:

      1. If the main product is damaged, exchange it for a new main product

    1. First month from date of purchase: free

    2. Month 2 to month 12: fee 10% of invoice value/month.

      3. (For example: 2nd month fee 10%, 3rd month fee 20%...)

      Note: If the customer does not have the main product to exchange, the Committed Warranty or Refund policy with a 50% discount will apply.

      2. If the included accessories are damaged, exchange them for accessories with similar functions that TopZone is selling

    1. Accompanying accessories can be exchanged for free within 12 months from the date of purchase of the main product with new PKAAK accessories being sold with equivalent quality.

      2. Note: If there are no compatible conditions or the Customer is not suitable, the manufacturer's warranty will apply

      3. Software errors do not apply, but only software error fixes

      4. In case the Customer wants to exchange the entire box (full box, original box):

      In addition to the return fee in Section 2.1, the customer will pay an additional fee for a full box equivalent to 20% of the invoice value.

      CONDITIONS APPLY

    1. Returned products must retain 100% of their original shape and qualify for the company's warranty.

    2. The body does not scratch the screen (applies to iPhone, iPad, iMac, Mac, MacBook)

    3. The product is for personal use only, the use of the product for commercial purposes is not applicable.


    • REFUND: Applicable to defective and non-defective products.

    1. First month from purchase date: fee 20% of invoice value

    2. Month 2 to month 12: fee 10% of invoice value/month.

      CONDITIONS APPLY

    1. Returned products must remain 100% in their original form and qualify for the company's warranty.

    2. The body does not scratch the screen (applies to iPhone, iPad, iMac, Mac, MacBook, Apple Watch)

    3. The product is for personal use only, commercial use of the product is not applicable.

      4. Full refund of ink cartridges and accompanying accessories:

    4. Loss of box charges 2% of invoice value for iPhone, iPad, iMac, Mac, MacBook, Apple Watch groups

    5. Lost accessories will be charged according to the minimum selling price on TopZone and genuine websites (maximum increase of 5% of invoice value) for all product groups.

      6. Refund all promotions. If the discount is lost, a fee will be charged according to the published price.
